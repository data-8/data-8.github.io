Announcements-06-2018

Mon 18 June 2018
By The Course Staff
  • Welcome to the start of the semester! Please sign up for Piazza. We'll use it to answer questions, post announcements, and more.
  • Lab starts this week -- bring a laptop. You can borrow one for the semester from the library if you don't have your own!
  • Create your computing account by going to summer.datahub.berkeley.edu and logging in with your @berkeley.edu account. Try starting your server; it takes about a minute. If you have problems, let us know by asking questions in lab or posting on Piazza.
