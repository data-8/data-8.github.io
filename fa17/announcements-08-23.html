Announcements-08-23

Wed 23 August 2017
By The Course Staff

  • Lab starts this week. Bring a laptop. You can borrow one for the semester from the library if you don't have your own. See Piazza for details.

  • If you couldn't sign up for labs, try again; we added extra space.

  • Sign up for one of our connector courses.

  • Please sign up for Piazza. We'll use it to answer questions, post announcements, and more.

  • Create your computing account by going to datahub.berkeley.edu and logging in with your @berkeley.edu account. Try starting your server. It takes about a minute. If you have problems, let us know by coming to lab and asking questions or posting on Piazza.
