Fri 24 January 2020
By The Course Staff
  • The second lecture (on 11/24) will be held in Zellerbach Hall from 10am-11am: all students, including those on the waitlist, are welcome to attend.
  • HW1 will be relased today, Friday, January 24 in the Assignment column of the table below. It will be due on Thursday, January 30, and you can submit it a day early for one point of extra credit.

  • If you have a conflict with the midterm exam due to athletics, academic obligations, religious holidays, etc., please fill out this form as soon as possible.
